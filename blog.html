Story Cum Article On Father

There was a family,a middle class family.Where there are husband,wife and a son.There was a small provision store of that man.There were many things like wafer packets(10 rupess),chocolates of (2 to 50 rupess),sampoo pack(2 to 6 rupess),toothbrush(40-50 rupess) etc. He daily goes at shop at 8 in morning and comes back at 7 in evening.

One day asusal he goes to his shop.Sits there for a full day.(What you think how much he earns in a day ?? Lets check it).Let guess he sells 5 packets of wafer and he takes 10rupess for each(he gets that on 6 rupess) so 5*4=20 Rs. Profit. Also sells 15 choclate of 3 rupess(he gets on rs. 1.5) so profit is 1.5*15=22.5.Also sell two toothbrushes of rs. 45(he gets that at rs. 30) so profit is 15*2=30 rs. Let also guess that he also sells something more and earns another 40rs.So final profit of a day is 20+23(22.5)+30+40=Rs. 113.

Now he comes to home in evening. He and his son (who was in college) sits together on dining table. Son tells his father a one santance that “Give me Rs. 140 because I want it for my internet pack.” Father doesn’t think for a second and gives him 140 rs.(Which was like earning of 1.5 days at shop).

After some days at evening father was happy because he earned Rs.200 on that day.He thought that I will buy pavbhaji today(of 150-175 Rs.). He comes home with pavbhaji.Father and son was talking,son told father that “Give me 250 because our friend circle is going for party in hotel after long time. Father told politly that “My son, you can go with your friends after some day,today I have bought pavbhaji for us so lets enjoy it!!” Son becomes angry and told father that he never permits for a party!! After he goes to his room without eating pavbhaji.(Just think what will be the effect of this in father’s mind)Father goes to sons room and gives him 250 Rs. From his savings.!!

Just think tention on fathers mind..This is just a routin problem of son. (There is also son’s fees of college,his expenses of cloths,his daily problems of buying stationaries,zerox etc.) There is also responsibility of family’s food,Family health care problem,Expenses on petrol, maintenance of vehical,electricity bills etc.

If we guess that that provision shop is somewhat famous and he earns 200-250rs. Per month than also this problem is common.!!

How much tensions are there in a father’s mind. We sons expenses many rupess in thing which is not necessary. At time of our expenses just think about this story and think about hard work of father in a day. Also think that how much father expenses for him self?? I think 1/4th part of our expenses. Most of father uses mobile of Rs.2000-2500 and son uses mobile of 10000-15000(Someone also of 20000-25000 !).
